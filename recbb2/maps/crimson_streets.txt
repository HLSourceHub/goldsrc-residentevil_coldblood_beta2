============================
Resident Evil : Cold Blood
============================

Streets


Welcome to the Crimson Head mode of RE:CB Beta 2! Here player is pitted against player,
zombie vs human. A player is picked at random to be the Crimson Head; the rest are your
chosen character. The goal is simple: Hunt down the Crimson Head if you're human and hunt
down the humans if you're the Crimson Head! Use your lives wisely, because once they're gone,
you're out of the game.


Happy hunting!
